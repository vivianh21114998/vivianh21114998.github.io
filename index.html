Lifehouse Band

Lifehouse is a rock band, with Jason Wade as the lead singer and guitarist, Rick Woolstenhulme Jr. as the drummer,
and Bryce Soderberg as the bassist. They were most famous during the 2000s, but they are still creating music. As of 2018,
their most recent released album was called The Wastelands.

Early History

The band was founded in 1995 in Los Angeles. Their first successful album was No Name Face in 2000.
The band's first song to reach the Billboard chart was Hanging by a Moment,
which was the third song in chart history
to be named the "No.1 Song of the Year."
